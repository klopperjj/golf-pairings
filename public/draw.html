Day 1 · Thursday
Scramble Drive
Four-Ball Better Ball Match Play
Both partners tee off. The best drive is selected — both players then play their own ball from that spot for the remainder of the hole. The better net score of the pair contests each hole. Holes are won, halved, or lost. Strokes received per stroke index at 85% of course handicap.
Day 2 · Friday
Normal Play
Four-Ball Better Ball Match Play
Each player plays their own ball throughout. The better net score of the two partners contests each hole against the opposing pair. Holes are won, halved, or lost. Strokes received per stroke index at 85% of course handicap.
